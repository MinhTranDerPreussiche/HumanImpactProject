Hi, I'm Minh

I stopped eating beef for breakfast for 10 days to reduce my carbon footprint.
Here's how I did it:

Day 1 Breakfast

On the first day, I ate Hawaiian bread for breakfast. It was quite simple, and it helped me get to school quickly because I didn't need to prepare much. I still came late though.

Day 2 Breakfast

This morning, I made Udon noodles. But this time was special, I removed the beef from the recipe, and it still tastes good.

Day 3 Breakfast

I made Udon for breakfast again.

Day 4 Breakfast

Yep. You guessed it, more Udon. I also added an egg on top! I think that even without the beef, the egg definitely brought in a lot more flavour to spice things up a bit. I'm done with Udon for now.

Day 5 Breakfast

This is some bread from publix. I bought it at their "deli" and I think it tastes pretty good. I air fried it for 5 minutes with the heat dial at two hours.

Day 6 Breakfast

I ate some chicken instant noodles today. I remember it was actually pretty good for how easy it was to make. I wouldn't eat this everyday though.

Day 7 Breakfast

I ate more bread today, but tried a different type, walnut. I was in a bit of a rush so I didn't remember air frying it that day. I shared it with my Dad on the car ride to school

Day 8...

No breakfast... I woke up too late!

Day 9 Breakfast

I tried whole grain bread and didn't really like it. It was very hard, perhaps I air fried it too long.

Day 10 Breakfast

Something interesting today. My mother made a vietnamese jelly snack, I didn't really like it but I'm glad I tried it out.
